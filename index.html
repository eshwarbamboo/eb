Hello there!

We are Yenugandula.

EshwarBamboos is a Yenugandula's business specializing in bamboo crafts for over 30 years. With a commitment to quality and custom designs, we complete more than 500 projects annually, catering to a variety of occasions.

Our services encompass creating stunning pelli pandiri, stages for events, Ganesh and Durga Devi mandapalu, yagnam pandiri, and functional structures like sheds and fences. We also provide elegant flower decorations and garlands, along with car flower decorations for special events..

Additionally, we supply bamboo ballies for house construction, making us a trusted name in the bamboo craft industry.
